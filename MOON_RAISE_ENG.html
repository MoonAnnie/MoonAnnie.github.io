ABOUT MOON RAISE

MOONRAISE is a well-lit party room, and each space is uniquely configured so you can enjoy it in a variety of concepts.
MOONRAISE is a party room suitable for various purposes such as studio shoots, birthday parties, bridal showers, baby showers,
year-end parties, and group gatherings.
MOONRAISE is located a 5-minute walk from 'Mullae Creative Village', where there are many great restaurants,
so you can enjoy food from famous restaurants without waiting in a long line.

🌟 As an open event, the party balloon package will be provided free of charge for one month. 🌟

RESERVATION 💰


- MOONRAISE can be reserved for three times 'Day ☀️ / Night 🌰 / All Night 🌚'.

- The standard number of people is 6 and the maximum is 8. If there are more than 7 people, 10,000 won will be added per person.

- A deposit of 50,000 won will be refunded to your personal account after cleaning and recycling after using the facility.

- Smoking is prohibited indoors, and pets are not allowed.

- A security camera is installed in front of the exterior door to check in and out and the number of people, and if illegal use is detected,
you will be asked to leave without a refund.❗️

- Day ☀️ -

• Time : 11 ~ 16
• Price : ₩ 60,000
• Number of People : 6 people (+2 people)

- Night 🌰 -

• Time : 19 ~ 24
• Price : ₩ 80,000
• Number of People : 6 people (+2 people)

- All Night 🌚 -

• Time : 19 ~ +8
• Price : ₩ 120,000
• Number of People : 6 people (+2 people)
